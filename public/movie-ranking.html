The Best Movies According to Praveen Kumar

My top 5 movies of all-time

Dead Poets Society (1989)

Maverick teacher John Keating returns in 1959 to the
prestigious New England boys' boarding school where he was
once a star student, using poetry to embolden his pupils
to new heights of self-expression.

Good Will Hunting (1997)

Will Hunting, a janitor at M.I.T., has a gift for mathematics,
but needs help from a psychologist to find direction in his life.

Oppenheimer (2023)

The story of American scientist, J. Robert Oppenheimer, and
his role in the development of the atomic bomb.

The Wolf of Wall Street (2013)

Based on the true story of Jordan Belfort, from his rise
to a wealthy stock-broker living the high life to his fall
involving crime, corruption and the federal government.

Shutter Island (2010)

Teddy Daniels and Chuck Aule, two US marshals, are sent
to an asylum on a remote island in order to investigate the
disappearance of a patient, where Teddy uncovers a shocking truth
about the place
